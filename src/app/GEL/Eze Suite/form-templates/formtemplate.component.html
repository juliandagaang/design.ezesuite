• Form templates

    General rules

    • Keep the visual direction vertical (top to bottom), not left to right.
    • All form fields should have clear labels.
    • Fields can have suggested texts.
    • Fields can have notes/descriptions added above or below the field.
    • Fields can be visually grouped together. Each group should have a clear title.
    • Buttons are positioned at the bottom left of the page.
